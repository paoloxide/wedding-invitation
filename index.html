What is an Unplugged Wedding?

We request everyone to keep the ceremony camera-free. While our I Do's are unplugged, our reception is not and definitely as a couple who loves pictures, you'll get tons of options to take your pictures. We prepared for this event wholeheartedly. Please use our official hashtag: #JasonAndMhyWedding2025

Is RSVP really needed before going?

Yes, please. We will be needing your formal RSVP to consolidate guest details and finalize the headcount for catering and seating purposes. We are so excited to celebrate our wedding day with you! To ensure an intimate and enjoyable experience for everyone, we have reserved seating for each guest individually. We kindly ask that each invitation is for ONE (1) PERSON ONLY.

Please RSVP by February 14, 2025 to confirm your attendance. We can't wait to share this unforgettable day with you!

What should I wear for the wedding?

Attire: Strictly Formal

Male: Black suit partnered with either a bow or a neck tie

When is the appropriate time to leave?

It took us 12 years to get to this point of our lives, and we want to celebrate it with the people that are very dear to our hearts. We want you to have fun! Celebrate with us until the end of the program!

Can I bring my kids to the wedding?

Unfortunately, this is an ADULTS-ONLY event with the exception of the entourage.

May I invite a plus one to the event?

As much as we would love to accommodate all our friends and family, we have a limited number of guests. Please understand that this event is strictly by invitation only. Kindly check our invitation to know the number of seats allotted for you. Guests not found on the guestlist provided will not be allowed to enter.

How far is the Church from the Reception?

Stalla Suites Events Place is appropriximately 4 kilometers away from the Immaculate Conception Cathedral of Cubao, which is less than 15 minutes away by car.

Can I sit anywhere at the reception?

No. Our coordinators will gladly assist you in finding your designated seat after registration. Feel free to ask them for assistance and they will gladly help you.

How can I help the couple have a good time?

  • Keep the ceremony camera-free by following the Unplugged Wedding rule.
  • Pray with us for favorable weather and the continuous blessings of our Lord as we enter this new chapter of our lives as husband and wife.
  • RSVP as soon as your schedule is cleared.
  • Dress appropriately and follow our wedding motif.
  • Be on time.
  • Follow the seating arrangement in the reception.
