Wedding Events

Here are the events that will take place on our wedding day. Please take note that this is an Unplugged Wedding. We would like to ask our guests to refrain from using their phones during the ceremony.

March 15, 2025
Quezon City, Philippines

12:00 PM - Church Assembly & Ceremony

40 Lantana, Cubao
Guests are expected to gather at the Immaculate Conception Cathedral of Cubao for the wedding ceremony.

02:00 PM - Church Photograph Opportunity

40 Lantana, Cubao
After the wedding ceremony, guests will be called for a photo opportunity with the newlyweds.

03:30 PM - Reception Assembly

1008 Quezon Ave Diliman
Guests are expected to gather at the Stalla Suites Events Place for the reception program.

04:00 PM - Reception Program Proper

1008 Quezon Ave Diliman
The reception program will commence with the entrance of the newlyweds and their entourage.

06:00 PM - Dinner and Party

1008 Quezon Ave Diliman
The reception program will end with a dinner and party for all of the guests.
